# Sales Trend Dashboard — Power BI

Interactive 2-page Power BI dashboard analyzing $484K in retail 
sales across categories, regions, and time periods.

## Business Problem
Sales managers need a single view to monitor revenue trends, 
identify top-performing regions, and spot underperforming periods.
This dashboard enables data-driven decisions on inventory, 
staffing, and regional strategy.

## Tools Used
- Power BI Desktop — dashboard and visualization
- Dataset: Retail Sales Transaction Data (2014)

## Dashboard Highlights

### Page 1 — Sales Trend
- Total Sales: $484K | Profit: $49,544 | Orders: 595
- Technology is the top category at $175.28K
- California leads all states at $91.30K in sales
- Weekday sales (352.98K) outperform weekends (131.27K) by 2.7x
- October is the peak month at 81.78K orders
- Thursday has the lowest daily orders (24) — a clear operational insight

### Page 2 — Profit & Customer Analysis
- New York leads in profit ($13.75K) despite California 
  leading in sales — margin difference worth investigating
- Phones ($77.39K) and Chairs ($77.24K) are top sub-categories
- Technology generates highest profit ($22.59K) vs 
  Furniture ($5.46K) despite similar sales volumes
- Top customers identified by individual sales contribution

## Features
- Year and Month slicers for dynamic filtering
- 2-page navigation with drill-down capability
- Treemap, donut chart, bar and line chart visuals
